Pyramids of Giza, Egypt

The Pyramids of Giza are the last surviving wonder of the ancient world and stand as a testament to the incredible engineering feats of the ancient Egyptians. They were built during Egypt’s Fourth Dynasty of the Old Kingdom (c. 2580–2560 BCE), primarily as tombs for the pharaohs and their queens.
