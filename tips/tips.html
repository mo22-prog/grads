Essential Travel Tips

Your friendly guide to navigating Egypt like a local — smart, safe, and stylish.

Top Tips for Travelers

🧍 Respect Local Customs

Dress modestly in mosques and rural areas. Light, breathable clothing is best for the climate.

💧 Stay Hydrated

The desert heat is real! Always carry bottled water and avoid drinking tap water.

💰 Cash is King

Carry small bills in Egyptian pounds. Most small vendors don't accept cards.

🛺 Know Your Transport

Use Uber in cities, negotiate taxi fares in advance, and try the local microbuses!

📱 SIM Cards & Connectivity

Grab a local SIM (Vodafone or Orange) at the airport for cheap data and smooth GPS navigation.

🎟️ Tickets & Tips

Carry student IDs for discounts. Tipping (baksheesh) is expected for small services.

Safety First

  • 🚨 Avoid isolated areas at night.
  • 👮 Trust tourist police in major sites.
  • 📍 Share your itinerary with someone back home.
  • 🩺 Travel with basic meds for heat & digestion.
