Things to Do in Egypt

Dive in the Red Sea

Explore coral reefs and marine life in Sharm El Sheikh or Hurghada.

Desert Safari

Ride camels, enjoy Bedouin dinners, and stargaze under clear skies.

Visit Ancient Temples

Discover the temples of Karnak, Luxor, Abu Simbel and more.

Try Egyptian Food

Indulge in Koshary, Fatta, Mahshi and fresh desserts like basbousa.

Nile River Cruise

Relax on a floating hotel between Luxor and Aswan with guided tours.

Climb Mount Sinai

Hike before sunrise to experience breathtaking views and history.
